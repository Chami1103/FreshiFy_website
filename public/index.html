Project Overview

FreshiFy integrates IoT sensor networks with advanced machine learning algorithms to create a comprehensive food management solution. The system monitors environmental conditions in real-time, predicts food spoilage, analyzes storage compatibility, and provides personalized recipe recommendations to minimize waste.

Global Context: Food waste is a critical global challenge, with approximately 1.03 billion tonnes wasted annually, contributing to economic losses and environmental degradation.

Sri Lankan Context: The tropical climate accelerates food spoilage, leading to 20‚Äì30% of purchased perishables being wasted in households due to inadequate storage practices and outdated tracking methods.

Current Solutions: Existing food monitoring systems lack real-time adaptability and fail to address critical storage interactions, such as ethylene-producing fruits affecting sensitive items like leafy greens.

üîç Research Gap

Our comprehensive analysis revealed several critical limitations in current food storage systems:

‚Ä¢ Real-Time Monitoring: Absence of continuous environmental factor tracking (temperature, humidity, ethylene gas exposure)
‚Ä¢ Personalization: Lack of user-specific recommendations based on dietary preferences and health profiles
‚Ä¢ Compatibility Analysis: No intelligent system to identify harmful food storage combinations
‚Ä¢ Climate Adaptation: Inadequate dynamic shelf-life predictions for tropical environments
‚Ä¢ User Experience: Limited intuitive interfaces for household adoption

‚ùì Research Problem

"How can we develop an intelligent, IoT-enabled system that dynamically monitors food storage environments, accurately predicts spoilage in tropical climates, analyzes food compatibility, and provides personalized recommendations to significantly reduce household food waste?"

1

2

6

Ensure modular architecture and user-friendly mobile interface for widespread adoption
